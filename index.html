SheInnovates

SheInnovates is a tool to help improve your English speaking skills. Simply record yourself and SheInnovates will catch any grammatical errors

Here's a question to help you get started:
Question: What's your favorite animal?
Record Your Audio
  • Record yourself by clicking and holding the 'Record' button
  • When you are done recording, release the record button
  • To submit the recording, hit the 'Submit' button
